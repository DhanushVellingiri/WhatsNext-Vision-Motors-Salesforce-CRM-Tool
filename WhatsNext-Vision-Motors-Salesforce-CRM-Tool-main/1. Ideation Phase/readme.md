# Phase 1: Ideation Phase

## Project Overview
The "WhatsNext Vision Motors CRM" is a specialized Salesforce application designed to modernize the operations of a multi-branch automotive dealership. 

## Problem Statement
Traditional car dealerships often struggle with disconnected systems. Inventory management is manual, assigning online customers to local physical dealerships is inefficient, and tracking post-sale activities (like Test Drives and Service Requests) often falls through the cracks, leading to poor customer experience.

## Proposed Solution
Build a centralized, automated CRM on the Salesforce platform. The system will:
1. Track comprehensive data for Customers, Dealerships, and Vehicle Inventory.
2. Automate the vehicle ordering process by utilizing geolocation mapping to assign customers to their nearest physical dealership automatically.
3. Prevent inventory errors by enforcing real-time stock validation.
4. Enhance the customer experience through automated communication workflows for test drives and service requests.
